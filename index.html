HOW IT WORKS

A learning loop that mirrors clinical practice.

01

Choose a Case

Browse 50 real fundus images across 10 conditions. Filter by difficulty level — Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced. Each case includes the original image, AI-generated teaching notes, and key findings to identify.

02

Analyze with AI Guidance

Work through a guided diagnostic pathway. The AI overlay highlights regions of interest using Grad-CAM heatmaps. Identify biomarkers, note the disc, vessels, and macula, and form your differential diagnosis.

03

Submit Your Diagnosis

Enter your top condition, rate your confidence level, and submit. The system compares your answer to the AI's structured output and scores your accuracy, specificity, and calibration.

04

Review, Track, Repeat

Get immediate feedback with clinical explanations. Your results feed into your dashboard — updating your accuracy score, confidence calibration, and adaptive case recommendations. Complete quizzes to reinforce your learning.

Cherry red spot · Ischemic whitening
